My name is Matt Vogel

When I was in high school, I took lots of computer classes and got my first introduction to programming with C++ back in 1999. We even had a computer programming team at our school, and during my junior year a few of my classmates and I went to Indianapolis once to compete against other schools in a coding competition. I enjoyed programming but I was also an avid athlete, playing soccer and basketball, which had my interest at the time and took me out of the coding arena for quite some time.

Fast forward to today. I have been a college soccer coach for the last 8 years, but have found myself wanting to get back into programming in my free time. I decided to start studying Python as I had heard it was an easy language to learn, and had been gaining lots of popularity. Within the first week I was hooked! Since then I've explored other technologies in the field and have been taking computer science classes at the college where I work! I am on my way to adding a CS degree to my existing double major there.

Below is an outline of what I bring to the table. I am a hardworker, pick up on things quickly, and am friendly and good with people. I am looking to pursue opportunities in the Software Development field and I hope you find this site helpful in your search for the right fit!
