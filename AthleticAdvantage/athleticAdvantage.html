College Preparatory Program

For soccer players looking to play at the college level

Who's It For?

The Collge Preparatory Program (CPP) is a training program and playing experience designed to develop players for college soccer. This program is run by college coaches and is specifically designed to teach players to tactically read the game and execute the best decisions and skills at speed - abilities college coaches value most.

Program Benefits:
  • Become a more attractive recruiting prospect to college coaches across the country
  • Get more than just general skill training... LEARN how to play with speed and think like a player at the next level
  • Learn college tactics and experience college training sessions from college coaches BEFORE you get to college - competitive advantage
  • Bridge the gap between club soccer ending and high school season starting
  • Get elite training to prepare for high school tryouts or college preseasons
  • Exposure to college coaches and their network of contact
  • Full field outdoor training
  • Can also be done to supplement your club soccer training

    • Each program has a player camp of 40 players, for high school grades and graduating seniors, to ensure that all participants are receiving adequate play and the highest quality of instruction.

    Topics Covered

    May be adjusted depending on the needs of the players in the program

  • Reading the game - Effective Principles of Play
  • Speed of Play
  • Defending
  • Crossing and Finishing
  • Possesion with purpose
  • Chance creation in the final third
  • Finishing and goal scoring
  • Pressing
  • Systems of Play
  • Transition from attack to defense
  • Transition from defense to attack
  • Goalkeeper topics -- Specific to the goalkeepers who attend training but may include: Shot Stopping, Distribution, Dealing with Crosses, Set Piece Organization, Owning the Box, Positioning and Footwork
